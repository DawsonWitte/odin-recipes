Chug Jug Recipe

Description

This is litterally the Chug Jug from Fortnite!! I don't even play Fortnite, but Chug Jug = funny.

Yeah... I have a weird sense of humor.


Ingrediants

  • Green Hawaiin Punch or Mountain Dew
  • Sugar Free or Low Sugar Blue Gatorade or Powerade
  • Blue Food Coloring
  • Ice

Instructions

  1. Put a few drops of the blue food colouring into the gatorade bottle and shake to blend. This will give the blue a more intense colour.
  2. Fill your glass with ice.
  3. Fill the glass half full with the green Hawaiian Punch or Mountain Dew
  4. Slowly pour in the blue gatorade. You'll want to be very gentle here and can even use a spoon to help slow the pour.
  5. Toss in a straw and enjoy!
